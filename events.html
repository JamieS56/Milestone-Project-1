Bournemouth beach run

Our first event back in 2016 was a 5km beach run where people got sponserships from there friends and family to do the race. It lasted a total of 5 hours for everyone to complete the race and in total we raised £4,324 and had 352 participnts.

Dolphin Raffle

In 2017 we held an online raffle to win a dolphin bundle that included many dolphin themed items like soft toys pencils, candles and other items. we had over 2,000 people enter our contest through our website. We raised over £3000 from the raffle with entry to the raffle costing minimum £1 entry.

Sposored swim

In 2018 we held a sponsored swim in which people had to swim 10km. they could do it wherever they wanted, in there local swiming pool, in the sea. we had our biggest participation ever with over 5,000 people taking part. through the sponsored swim, we raised £24,322.
