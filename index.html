Welcome to Save the Seas!

We are Save the seas a charity dedicated to helping out all marine life and conserving our oceans.

About Us

We have been helping out sea life for 5 years now. We are based in the UK but have since grown and now have projects globally to protect and take care of marine life.

Women scuba diving

Why Help?

The seas are a vital part of the earths eco system, without them the there would be very little life on earth and we need to keep the seas healthy to sustain ourselves now.

How to help

There are many ways you can help. you can donate, take part in a fundraiser or do something as siimple as picking up litter from the beach. any small action is a step closer to protecting our seas.
